        How to use:

        1. Add players using the '+' button in the top right corner
        2. Select players from the dropdown or use 'Select All Players'
        3. Adjust player ratings if needed
        4. Click 'Balance Teams' to create two balanced teams
        5. Use 'Share Teams' to share the results
        6. Click 'Save' to store your current setup for next time
